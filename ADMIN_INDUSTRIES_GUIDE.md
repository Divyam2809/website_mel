# Admin Content Guide: Industries Section

This guide explains how to properly format text when adding or editing **Industries** in the Admin Panel, ensuring that lists and special sections display correctly on the website.

Some fields in the Admin Panel populate formatted lists on the website (like "Who Uses", "Use Cases", etc.). To maintain the layout, please follow these specific input formats.

---

### 1. Target Audience
*Appears in the "Who uses our education VR labs" section.*

*   **Format:** Enter each audience group on a **new line**.
*   **Note:** The numbers (01, 02, etc.) are generated automatically. Do not type them.

**Example Input:**
```text
K‚Äì12 schools (CBSE, ICSE, State Boards)
Junior colleges and degree colleges

```

---

### 2. Problems Solved
*Appears in the "What problems VR solves" section.*

*   **Format:** Enter each problem statement on a **new line**.

**Example Input:**
```text
Limited physical lab access
Safety risks in experiments
Low student engagement
Concept memorization without understanding
```

---

### 3. Use Cases
*Appears in the "Use cases" section with Badges.*

*   **Format:** Use the pipe symbol `|` to separate the **Badge**, **Title**, and **Description**.
*   **Syntax:** `BADGE | Title Text | Description Text`

**Example Input:**
```text
LAB | Science lab simulations | Conduct virtual experiments safely without physical lab constraints
MATH | Mathematics and geometry visualization | Visualize complex 3D shapes and mathematical concepts interactively
EXPLORE | History and geography immersion | Explore historical events and geographical locations in immersive VR
CAREER | Career and skill exploration | Experience different careers and develop practical skills virtually
```

---

### 4. Stats
*Appears in the bottom statistics grid.*

*   **Format:** Use the pipe symbol `|` to separate the **Value** and the **Label**.
*   **Syntax:** `Value | Label`

**Example Input:**
```text
120+ | Schools
50K+ | Students
75% | Better Retention
```

---

### 5. Other Fields

*   **Modal Title**: The main large heading inside the popup modal.
*   **Full Summary**: The introductory paragraph inside the popup modal.
*   **Summary (Main)**: The short description shown on the Industry Card on the main page.
*   **Impact**: A short impact statement (sometimes used in the generic modal or card details).
